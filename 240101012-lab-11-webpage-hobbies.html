My Hobbies

I'm an avid fan of cinema and enjoy exploring different genres, from thought-provoking science fiction to classic dramas. Following a well-crafted story is one of my favorite ways to unwind.

I love the strategic depth of chess. It's a fantastic mental exercise that teaches patience, foresight, and problem-solving. I enjoy playing games online and studying different openings and tactics.
